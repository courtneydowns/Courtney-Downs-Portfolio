About Me

Greetings! My name is Courtney Downs. I am an animal lover and a Harry Potter super fan. I own at trivia nights around town! Ravenclaw all the way. Don't worry if you're not in Ravenclaw. I believe every house is just as vital to the success of Hogwarts.

I am from southwestern Indiana and a proud Hoosier, but I've lived all over the country -- from Vermont to New Orleans to Florida to Tennessee and back to Indiana. I've settled back into being a Hoosier, and am loving every second of it. While in Florida, I graduated in 2014 from Lynn University with a Bachelor's in Psychology.

I graduated from Eleven Fifty Academy's Web Development program in November 2021, where I have learned a multitude of skills that will make me an excellent addition to any team. I pride myself on being a team player, and truly believe that being a part of a successful team is more gratifying than going it alone.

Please take a look at my Github, Linkedin and resume. You will see that I am a well-rounded individual with a variety of work experiences. I like to think that I'm like a swiss army knife -- dependable, functional and adaptable.

Credo

01
Imagine

Phenominal projects or development doesn't start with a plan. It starts with imagining the unimaginable and laying out the path to make ideas come to life.

02
Enthusiasm

Enthusiam drives a project. Without enthusiasm, there is no heart, and without the heart of the project, it withers and dies before it really begins.

03
Detail Oriented

Attention to detail is a must. Finding a needle in a haystack is vital when creating and working on a project.

04
Dedication

With any project or new endeavor, it is essential to be invested 100%. Even when the going gets tough, I don't let my dedication waver. I see it through until I reach the end of the project.

05
Communication

When workin as a part of a team, it is essential to be able to communicate. If communication breaks down, so does the project. I pride myself on my interpersonal skills and excellent written and verbal communication.

06
Teamwork

There is a saying that "Teamwork makes the dream work." I have found that to be true in every project I have ever worked on, be it professionally or academically. It is much more efficient and satisfying to work on a project as a team as opposed to going it alone.

07
Strive for Perfection

When I am working on a project, I always strive for perfection; however, I know that I will never do something perfect, with no errors. That being said, I believe that by striving for perfection, the end result will be leaps and bounds better than if I had strived for good enough.

08
Success

The end result, with teamwork and the rest of the credo mentioned above, will have the end result be successful every time.
